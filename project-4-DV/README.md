# Project 4 – Data Visualization & Storytelling

## Overview

This project focuses on transforming raw e-commerce sales data into meaningful visual insights using Python. Various charts and graphs were created to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and business metrics. The visualizations help in understanding patterns and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objectives

- Load and explore the e-commerce sales dataset.
- Perform basic data preprocessing.
- Create informative visualizations using Python.
- Analyze sales trends and customer behavior.
- Present business insights through data storytelling.

---

## Business Insights

### Revenue by Product

- Compared revenue generated by each product.
- Identified the products contributing the highest sales revenue.

### Monthly Revenue Trend

- Visualized monthly sales performance.
- Identified changes in revenue over time.

### Payment Method Distribution

- Analyzed customer payment preferences.
- Compared usage of different payment methods.

### Order Status Distribution

- Examined the number of Delivered, Shipped, Pending, Returned, and Cancelled orders.

### Referral Source Analysis

- Compared customer acquisition across different referral sources.
- Identified the most effective referral channels.

### Correlation Analysis

- Studied relationships between numerical variables.
- Found correlations among Quantity, Unit Price, Items in Cart, and Total Price.
